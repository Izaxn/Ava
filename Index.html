Who am I

Hi, I'm Eva

I'm a self-taught nail artist just starting this beautiful chapter — new to running a business, but not new to the craft I love. Every set I do, I'm learning, refining, and pouring genuine care into.

I believe good nail care doesn't need to be rushed or overhyped — just clean tools, patient hands, and a bit of colour to make your day better. Thank you for being part of this journey with me.

Before You Come In

A little routine to follow

Just a few small things that help your appointment go smoothly.

1

Bare nails, if you can

Arrive with polish-free nails where possible — I'll gently remove any old colour if not.

2

Skip the lotion

Avoid moisturiser or oils on your hands right before your visit.

3

Have a length in mind

A rough idea of your preferred shape and length helps us start faster.

4

Mention sensitivities

Let me know about any allergies or sensitive skin beforehand.

5

Arrive a little early

5 minutes early gives you time to settle in and pick your shade.
